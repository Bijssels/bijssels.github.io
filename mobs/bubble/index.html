Bubble

Summary:

Silly particle monster thats a family of the blaze/breeze as a "water" mob


Spawning:

Bubble spawns in the Dungeon trough bubble spawners

Attacks:

She has multiple attacks

  • Jump:
  • Goes up and places water below and does a shock attack at once
  • Shock:
  • Spawns a "shockwave" of water around her dealing damage
  • Spit:
  • Shoots a small projectile forward that will place water if hitting wall and deals damage and goes away when hitting entitie
  • Hit:
  • Hits the player like a zombie

Drops:

After defeat she has a 1 in 3 chance it will drop a bubble rod
