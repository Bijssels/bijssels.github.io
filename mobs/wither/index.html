Wither

Summary:

Wither got made harder with more health and more attacks

Health: 600HP (300 hearts)


Phases:

there are multible phases adding more attacks / having specific mecanics

  • Normal:
  • everything above 75% health
  • Special:
  • everything between 50% health and 75% health
  • Half:
  • everything between 30% health and 50% health
  • Mad:
  • everything between 25% health and 30% health
  • Weak:
  • everything below 25% health
  • Final:
  • everything below 100HP (50 hearts)

Attacks:

there are multible attacks

  • Normal:
  • every normal wither attack
  • always happenes
  • Wither Skeleton Spawn:
  • spawns 4 wither skeletons
  • happanes after Speical phase
  • Explode:
  • makes explotion in front of it
  • happanes after Speical phase
  • Dash:
  • dashes forwards destroying everything in its path
  • happanes after Half phase

Mecanics:

there are multible mecanics

  • Normal:
  • just normal wither
  • always happenes
  • Spawn:
  • makes lots of explotions
  • happenes when spawning
  • Mad:
  • 100% chance on custom attack
  • happenes when in Mad phase
  • Final:
  • moves away from clostes player
  • dashes more
  • happenes when in Final phase
